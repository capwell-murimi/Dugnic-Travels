Samburu National Reserve

22 tours

Located in Kenya's northern region, Samburu offers a unique safari experience with species not found in other parks, such as the reticulated giraffe, Grevy's zebra, and Somali ostrich.

Maasai Mara National Reserve

22 tours

Famous for its abundant wildlife and the annual wildebeest migration. Visitors can witness the "Big Five" (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhinoceros) and enjoy hot air balloon safaris.

Lake Nakuru National Park

22 tours

Famous for its pink flamingos, Lake Nakuru is also a sanctuary for rhinos and other wildlife. Visitors can enjoy game drives and birdwatching.

Lamu Island

22 tours

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Lamu is known for its well-preserved Swahili culture, beautiful beaches, and traditional dhows. Visitors can explore the narrow streets of Lamu Town and enjoy water activities.

Hells gate National Park

22 tours

Famous for its dramatic scenery, including towering cliffs, gorges, and hot springs. Visitors can hike, rock climb, and enjoy wildlife viewing in a unique landscape. Known for its lush forests, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife, including the rare bongo antelope. Visitors can stay in tree lodges and enjoy game viewing from elevated platforms.

Aberdare National Park

22 tours

Known for its lush forests, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife, including the rare bongo antelope. Visitors can stay in tree lodges and enjoy game viewing from elevated platforms.

Lake Turkana

22 tours

The largest desert lake in the world, Lake Turkana is surrounded by volcanic landscapes and is home to unique wildlife, including Nile crocodiles and various bird species.

Diani Beach

22 tours

Located on Kenya's south coast, Diani Beach offers pristine white sands, turquoise waters, and a range of water sports and activities, including snorkeling, diving, and dolphin watching.

Shaba National Reserve

22 tours

Part of the Ewaso Ng'iro ecosystem, Shaba is known for its rugged landscapes, diverse wildlife, and the Joy Adamson Camp, made famous by the book and film "Born Free.

Ol Pejeta Conservacy

22 tours

A leading conservation area in Kenya, Ol Pejeta is home to the last two northern white rhinos and a variety of other wildlife. Visitors can enjoy game drives, guided walks, and visits to the chimpanzee sanctuary.

Tsavo National Parks(east and west)

22 tours

Tsavo is one of the largest wildlife reserves in the world, offering diverse landscapes, including savannahs, hills, and volcanic cones. Visitors can spot elephants, lions, and rhinos.

Mt Kenya

22 tours

Africa's second-highest mountain offers breathtaking scenery, diverse flora and fauna, and opportunities for mountain climbing, trekking, and wildlife viewing.
